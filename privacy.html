Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 13, 2025

This Policy explains how C2C Variations (“C2C”, “we”, “us”) handles personal information under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth) and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs).

What we collect

  • Account details (name, business name, email, phone, ABN)
  • Document inputs (job details, scope, prices, client names/emails)
  • Usage data (pages viewed, device/browser info, approximate location)
  • Support communications (including WhatsApp messages)

How we use it

  • Provide and improve services (generate documents, store history, support)
  • Communicate with you (service messages, updates)
  • Comply with law and manage risk (audit logs, disputes)
  • With consent, showcase testimonials or case studies

Legal basis

Consent, performance of a contract, or legal obligation.

Third‑party services

We use third parties (e.g., WhatsApp, Stripe, analytics, hosting/CDN). Your information may be processed by them under their own terms and privacy policies.

WhatsApp

Our WhatsApp‑first workflow means some information you send is handled by WhatsApp (Meta). Avoid sending sensitive information in chat. If you do, you accept WhatsApp’s terms and privacy policy.

Cookies & analytics

We may use cookies and analytics to understand usage and improve the product. You can block cookies in your browser, but parts of the site may not function properly.

Storage, security & retention

We use reasonable safeguards (encryption in transit, access controls). No method is 100% secure. We keep information only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

Overseas disclosures

Some providers may store/process data outside Australia. Where practicable, we use providers with strong security and privacy controls.

Your rights

  • Access and correct your personal information
  • Opt out of non‑essential communications
  • Lodge a complaint with us or the OAIC
